Part 1 - Duanju (Short Dramas)

Introduction

Duanju is a type of popular Chinese micro-drama, designed specifically for vertical viewing on smartphones. Each episode is usually 1-3 minutes long and is available on platforms like TikTok, Rednote, and others

Key Features

  • Fast-paced: Storylines move quickly to keep viewers hooked.
  • Dramatic & addictive: Exaggerated plots, intense emotions, and satisfying "punchline" endings
  • Global popularity: Duanju has gone beyond China, attracting millions of users worldwide and generating massive content consumption

Part 2 - Labubu X Blind Box Craze

Introduction

Labubu is a quirky monster doll originally created by Hong Kong illustrator Kasing Lung. It was later launched by Pop Mart, and quickly became one of the most iconic blind box collectibles. Each Labubu figure features its signature toothy grin and comes in surprise packaging, making every purchase a mystery

Why It Became a Global Sensation

  • The thrill of mystery: The blind box format gives buyers the excitement of surprise and rarity.
  • Social media buzz: Platforms like TikTok and Instagram helped the trend explode worldwide.
  • Celebrity influence: Stars like Blackpink's Lisa, Rihanna, and Dua Lipa shared their collections, fueling even more hype
  • Record-breaking sales: In the first half of 2025, Labubu generated hundreds of millions of dollars in global sales

Part 3 - Baijiu Modern Transformation

Introduction

Baijiu is China's traditional strong liquor with a history spanning thousands of years. Known for its high alcohol content-often over 50%-it was once the centerpiece of banquets and formal toasts, symbolizing respect, hospitality, and celebration

Changing Drinking Habits

Younger generations see traditional Baijiu as too strong and too old-fashioned. But this is rapidly changing as Baijiu brands experiment with new flavors and lower-alcohol versions to win over young consumers:

  • Fruit-flavored Baijiu
  • Baijiu lattes combining coffee culture with tradition
  • Baijiu-based cocktails served in trendy bars
  • Baijiu ice cream blending dessert and alcohol for novelty appeal
